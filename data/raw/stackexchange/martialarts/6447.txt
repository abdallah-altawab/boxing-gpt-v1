# If clinching is technically illegal, why does it almost always go unpunished?

I understand why boxers clinch (although it's the main reason I get bored watching most heavyweight fights), but I just learned that it is technically illegal. However, I've literally never seen a boxer being penalized for clinching. I would expect that many fighters would say boxing would be much more difficult, if not impossible, if the rules against clinching were strictly enforced. So if it is illegal, why are penalties so rare (in pro fighting), and if it is necessary, why is it illegal?
