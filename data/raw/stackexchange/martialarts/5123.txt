# Eye protection Muay Thai

I train in Muay Thai for six months now including sparing. I have myopia (-5 / -4.75) and I will get a femto LASIK surgery soon. My doctor told me that I could resume sparring after 6 weeks as long as I do not get punched in the eyes but I know that I will and that will put me at risk for flap dislocation. What should I wear to protect my eyes? Are there Special glasses or a type of head gear I can get?

## Answer (score: 5)
Short answer: don't spar until your doctor says it's okay. Accidents happen.

## Answer (score: 3)
Dr. Cary M. Silverman, MD recommends here a set of " rec specs " the hard-framed sports glasses that you say on, e.g., basketball players. On the same page, Emil Chynn, MD recommends LASEK surgery instead, which apparently has a lower chance of flap dislocation.
