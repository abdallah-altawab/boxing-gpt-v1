# What is the name of this style? (Karate maybe?)

I've seen this video around a fair bit, but I can't find much background about who / what organization / style it is?

## Answer (score: 6)
The video is from the World Martial Arts Association which was setup by Michael T. Dealy in the early 1990s, Mike Dealy trained under Grandmaster Duk Sung Son . The style is Chung Do Kwan , a form of Tae Kwon Do with light or no-contact. The WMAA has a video channel on Youtube , but about a year ago all video's were removed, probably because of the criticism they received. The WMAA is often ridiculed on the Internet as being a McDojo/McDojang (for example see here , here and here ) More information: http://wmaa.com/about/ http://www.oneworldtaekwondo.com/html/history.html
