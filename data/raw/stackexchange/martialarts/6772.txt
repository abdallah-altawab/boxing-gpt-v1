# Practicing Muay Thai kicks with no partner

Can I place a Belly Pad on a tree and practice some body kicks, or it is not recommended? For example, the following: Combat Sports Dome Tech Belly Pad

## Answer (score: 6)
I'd use a heavy bag instead of a tree, but if it's what you've got then it's what you've got. I wouldn't throw full force kicks because a tree doesn't "give" even if it's padded.

## Answer (score: 2)
Yes, you can attach a belly pad to a tree and kick it, but I wouldn't. Bear in mind that, unless it's a very thin tree, it is likely to offer more resistance than you would get hitting a human being (or suitable substitute). This will increase the chance of injuring your leg. Rather than attaching a pad to the trunk of a tree, I would suggest hanging a punch bag from a branch instead. The advantage of this is that you can practice moving around the bag as well as strikes other than kicks. You also reduce the risk of hitting the trunk instead of the pad.
