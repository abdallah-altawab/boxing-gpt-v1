# Ten Chin or Tein Chien or something else?

Ten chin or Ttein Chien I'm not sure of transliterated spelling, is the footwork where one foot replaces another/one moves back and the opposite forward, It should one of the four fundamental primary footwork moves in aikido - irimi, tenkan, irimi tenkan, and "ten chine" Can someone provide the correct transliteration spelling and possibly link to online resource?

## Answer (score: 1)
Tenshin is doing an ayumi ashi step forwards followed by a small tenkan, moving offline and adjusting to face uke. It is the beginning footwork that is done in the aikikai version of yokomen uchi shihonage omote .
