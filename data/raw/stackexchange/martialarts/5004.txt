## Answer (score: 3)
Another thing you can do if you have a small mouth is to go the children/teen area of a large box sporting good store. They will have quite a few different sized mouthguards in various configurations for different aged athletes. As a personal recommendation, I really like the Shock Doctor brand mouthguards. They do have one model available in both youth and adult sizes that tapers down very low at the back, this might help with the RGR that you have.
