Kind: captions Language: en What's the biggest reason why most people don't improve at boxing? I see this all the time. People will be going in the gym and they'll be working on the heavy bag. They'll be working on trying to punch harder, trying to punch faster, try to counter punch better, try to get better footwork, doing everything in one round. The problem with that is if you're trying to focus on everything, the one thing that you need to improve on will not improve. Great example of this is footwork. You need to be working on a specific drills for each session. If you get your feet right, everything else will improve.
