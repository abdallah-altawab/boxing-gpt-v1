Kind: captions Language: en If you've been following us, you'll already be familiar with step shuffles. This is a more advanced move and harder to pull off. But when used correctly, it's extremely effective. To step shuffle, you first step on a diagonal. Then shift your weight to that foot and hop into a new position using a circular motion. You can do a front step shuffle stepping with the lead foot and then hopping by driving your back foot. Or a back step shuffle stepping with your rear foot crossing your feet and then hopping by driving your lead foot. The movement pattern is the same. The only difference is which foot initiates the step.
