Kind: captions Language: en When targeting the body, you must bend your legs and drop your hips so you hit the target at the same height you're aiming for, not reaching down from above. A common mistake is to swing down at the body from an upright position. When you do that, you leave your head completely exposed and moreover, you lose power on the shot as you are not bringing your body with the punch. Hitting the body effectively requires getting into the right position first. dropping levels so that the punch travels on a short direct line and lands with more speed and power while still being covered.
