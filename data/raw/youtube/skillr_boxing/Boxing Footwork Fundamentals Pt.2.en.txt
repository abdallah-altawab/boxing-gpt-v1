Kind: captions Language: en A very common mistake when training is to stay in place when attacking or defending. Many boxers practice striking and defending in a static manner. This is not realistic. In a fight, both boxers are constantly moving. You must train to be able to perform all these techniques while moving around the ring. &gt;&gt; So, a lot of you guys, they were just sitting there. A lot of times you got to punch and move. You got to walk with it. You see the coach won't tell you to do the combination, but it's good to walk with the punches, too.
