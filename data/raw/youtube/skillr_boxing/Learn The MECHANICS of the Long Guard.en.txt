[Applause] [Music] Body shots from that side are usually defended by stepping back, often while posting with the lead hand. &gt;&gt; On the other side, when the opponent throws lead hand shots from the same side as your rear hand, your rear hand will be used to catch, block, or parry any incoming shots. Body shots on this side can also be defended by stepping back and posting or by blocking/parrying with the rear hand. Offensively, this guard is mostly used from outside range.
