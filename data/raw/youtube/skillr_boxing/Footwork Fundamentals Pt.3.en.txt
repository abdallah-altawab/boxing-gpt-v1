Kind: captions Language: en When you start boxing, you should keep in mind this very important rule. The foot that is in the direction you want to go moves first. If you want to go left, your left foot goes first. If you want to go right, your right foot goes first. This way you are opening the stance and then closing it. This is the fundamental way to move as this way you are always in balance. If you close the stance first and then open it, there is a brief moment where both feet come together and you become unbalanced, therefore in an inefficient and risky position. landing some good shots.
