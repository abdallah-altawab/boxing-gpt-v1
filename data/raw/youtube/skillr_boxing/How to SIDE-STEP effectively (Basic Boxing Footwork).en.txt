Kind: captions Language: en Side steps are also taught early in boxing. To side step, you shift your weight onto one foot, then push off that foot to step in the opposite direction. If you're moving right, push off your left foot. If you're moving left, push off your right. The motion is similar to a step back. In this case, you can actually move in different lines. &gt;&gt; But the main idea with side steps is that you are not actually crossing your feet and are simply stepping to one side or the other.
