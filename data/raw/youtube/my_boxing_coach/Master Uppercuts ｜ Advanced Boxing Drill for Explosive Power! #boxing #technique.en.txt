Kind: captions Language: en Uppercuts and all hooks. I describe this as a bit more of an advanced drill, but give it a go. Nice and controlled. Keep it loose. Push off the back leg. Uppercut there. And then knock out. Key is try not to focus too much on power with the uppercut. The power will just come. Execute the shot well. Push off that leg. Pivot round. One, two. Drive off the front leg to drive power into those hooks. One, two.
